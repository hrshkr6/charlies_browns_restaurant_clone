Our Story

Serving New Jersey with over 50 years of experience, Charlie Brown’s Fresh Grill and Steakhouse offers our signature and aged hand-carved, slow-roasted prime rib, delicious USDA Choice steaks, juicy USDA Choice burgers, our famous Unlimited Farmer’s Market Salad Bar™, and so much more. Our philosophy is simple: to provide great food, exceptional value, and friendly service to each and every customer we serve. No matter where you call home, you can expect the same family-friendly restaurant atmosphere, great service and value, and delicious food you look forward to any day of the week.
